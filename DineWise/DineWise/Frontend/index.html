The Mission

Purpose
DineWise is a Restaurant Review and Rating Aggregation System designed to simplify restaurant evaluation in today’s fragmented digital landscape.
Challenge
Platforms like Zomato, Swiggy, and Google provide independent ratings, making it difficult for users to interpret scattered and inconsistent information.
Solution
Our system consolidates platform-wise ratings into a single, structured interface with automated average score calculation.
Impact
By eliminating app-switching, DineWise enhances efficiency and enables informed dining decisions.
Academic Context
Developed as a Mumbai University Engineering Mini Project, demonstrating the architecture of a multi-source web-based data aggregation system.
